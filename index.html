About Me

Kevin learned HTML in elementary school, then took every programming class he could through high school.

While freelancing through college Kevin started a nerdy apparel line called Leetcore & sold a few hundred t-shirts. Selling a creative, physical product that people wanted was insanely gratifying.

To handle his growing freelancing business he built a CMS he called The Pantry and licensed it to 100+ bands including multi-platinum artists Panic at the Disco.

Kevin developed print-shop management suite Run My Shop to help run MerchSpin and developed ecommerce solutions for specialty shops including Johnny Cupcakes and online stores including District Lines.

After becoming a shareholder District Lines & sister print-shop MerchSpin, Kevin developed merchandising for YouTube creators and signed exclusive merch deals with hundreds of top channels including Smosh & Jenna Marbles and 4 MCNs.

Kevin got into live events after co-founding an artist management company focused on YouTube talent and made the first ever YouTuber tour with CAA, second national youtuber tour with Live Nation.

In 2010 Kevin co-created & produced Playlist Live, a 3-day YouTube focused convention, and grew the event to 4,000+ attendees.

After a successful 2013 event Kevin left to work a number of developing side projects and launched Channel Totals, a stat totaling webapp for YouTube channels.

Kevin lives in Orlando, FL with his crafty, design blogger wife Sarah.
