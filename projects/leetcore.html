Leetcore

Back to Projects & Portfolio Companies

Apparel line for nerds started in 2004. Right when I started getting into then-current post-hardcore music I fell in love with work from designers and shirtrepreneurs Rob Dobi & Jason Oda (warning, flash site), so making a clothing line was the next logical step.

The line was centered around incredibly simple, nerdy designs such as <3 over your heart, cute right? Leetcore served as my first foray into merchandising and it was my first successful business, with orders shipped to nearly every state and a dozen countries. While my designs were silly and generally weak I found that selling a creative, physical product that people wanted was insanely gratifying.

Quick Facts

  • Sold a few hundred t-shirts & hoodies
  • Learned about merch printing & pricing
  • Gave me something to talk about with my crafty wife Sarah the first time we met
