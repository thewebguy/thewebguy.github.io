RoadMerch

Back to Projects & Portfolio Companies

Tour merch sales tracking and re-ordering system for tour managers. RoadMerch, like many useful webapps, replaces horrible systems of spreadsheets.

Bands on the road have known for nearly a decade that their revenues come from merch, not music. Some bands you pay to see don't even get paid to perform, and even those that do may not earn enough to cover the gas for their next date on the road. Merch is the stop-gap between earning a living and starving musicians, so making sure you have enough merch on the road is absolutely vital.

Until RoadMerch tour managers had to rely on huge spreadsheets & count-outs to manage their most important revenue stream, now they have a direct link with their print shop.

Quick Facts

  • Track nightly sales
  • Work one-on-one with sales rep
  • Tie-in with RunMyShop for easy reorders
