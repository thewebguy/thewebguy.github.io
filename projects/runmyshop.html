RunMyShop

Back to Projects & Portfolio Companies

SaaS that powers print shops. It was originally built to power MerchSpin but as functionality was added I repurposed it for use by just about any print shop anywhere. Beyond managing print orders Run My Shop also performs scheduling, CRM and punch clock functions.

Running a shop is hard, especially when it comes to scheduling. When you run one machine and have just a handful of employees a paper & clipboard system works just fine with a few boxes to check off. But when volume grows and you have weeks or months of orders to plan out you need can't just eyeball it anymore.

The RunMyShop calendar is its killer feature - the shop manager can see the status of every order at once and place them according to their due date and complexity.

Quick Facts

  • Multi-machine, shop capable
  • Drag & drop scheduling with unit, color, side count totaling
  • Full-service time clock system
  • Sales, labor & material cost accounting & reporting
