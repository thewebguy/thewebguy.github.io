We Have Girlfriends

Back to Projects & Portfolio Companies

Semi-animated comedy series recounting embarrasing true stories. In October 2011 Kyle Sibert, Mitchell Davis and I sat down and recorded an embarrassing account of my high school career to the iPhone voice recorder app. In the next 24 hours I put my less-than-stellar drawing skills to the test and completed our first episode.

As we watched the comments that rolled in and brought in a few guest storytellers it became clear that embarrassing stories provide a pretty unique and incredible way to bring people together.

The show is currently on indefinite hiatus.
