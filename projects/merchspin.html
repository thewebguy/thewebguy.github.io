MerchSpin

Back to Projects & Portfolio Companies

Apparel printing shop servicing the music industry and small businesses, sister company to District Lines. As a shareholder I helped to fine tune processes and generated significant sales revenue.

There is no shortage of books, strategies or software for managing production shops but anyone who has run one knows the need for custom solutions. For MerchSpin I built RunMyShop, a custom print-shop management suite that handled everything from job scheduling to payroll calculations. Before leaving MerchSpin I brought in clients totalling over $3 million in revenue.

Quick Facts

  • Printed for thousands of artists including gold & platinum sellers
  • Full service DTG & screen-printing services
  • Special services like custom packaging & labeling
