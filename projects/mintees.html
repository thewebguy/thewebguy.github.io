Mintees

Back to Projects & Portfolio Companies

Mintees is a t-shirt design and critique forum that is home to professional and aspiring designers. It's much more than a design forum and showcase, it's focus is specifically t-shirt design and has an excellent community.

When Emptees was slated to go dark I called up iconic designer Rob Dobi, one of the first and most respected contributors on the site and asked him if he would like to start a spiritual successor with us. With permission from the owner of the old site I began crawling their API and Mintees was born.

Quick Facts

  • 20,000 designers
  • 25,000 t-shirt designs
  • Community driven design job market
